For assistance or issue reporting, please use the [Open Ticket](https://lunetix.cloud/tickets) feature through the provided support portal. Email support is currently unavailable. All service requests and technical inquiries are processed exclusively through the ticket system to ensure structured, prompt, and well-documented handling.

Additionally, the support team is also available via [Discord](https://discord.com/) and [Support Mail ](mailto:support@lunetix.cloud)for initial communication, casual discussions, or non-technical questions. However, all official requests must still be submitted through the ticket system to ensure they are handled systematically according to standard operating procedures.
